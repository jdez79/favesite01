Welcome to Daryl Desmond's Art Studio

Daryl Desmond, an award-winning artist now based in South Carolina, has drawn inspiration from life in the rugged north and the coastal west. His work—powerful western portraits, musical Artists, and fantasy scenes in pencil and pastel chalk—is known for its emotion and detail. Worldwide shipping available.
